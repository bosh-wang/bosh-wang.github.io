Altimeter

  • Measures the height of an aircraft

Vertical Speed Indicator (VSI)

  • Indicate whether the aircraft is climbing, descending or in level flight

Airspeed Indicator (ASI)

  • White arc → flap operating range
  • Vs0 → lower limit of white arc → stalling speed or minimum steady flight speed in the landing configuration
  • VFE → upper limit of white arc → the maximum speed with flaps extended
  • Green arc → normal operating range of the aircraft
  • Vs1 → lower limit of green arc → stalling speed or minimum steady flight speed in maximum takeoff weight configuration
  • VN0 → maximum structural cruising speed
  • Yellow arc → fly within this range only in smooth air
  • VNE → red line → never exceed speed
