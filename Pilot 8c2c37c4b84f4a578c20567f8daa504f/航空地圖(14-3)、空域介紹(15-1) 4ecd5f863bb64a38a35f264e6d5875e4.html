航空地圖(14-3)

Sources of Airport Data

  • Aeronautical Charts
  • Chart Supplement US
  • Notices to Airmen (NOTAMs)
  • Automated Terminal Information Service (ATIS)

Airport Markings and Signs

  • Runway Safety Area (RSA)
  • Runway Safety Area Boundary Sign → to make sure that runway is clear
  • Runway Holding Position Sign
  • Runway Holding Position Marking

    When exiting the runway, no ATC clearance is required to cross the line.

  • Runway Distance Remaining Sign
    • 2 means 2000 feet of runway remaining
  • Runway Designation Marking
    • 2 runways → L and R
    • 3 runways → L, C and R
  • Land And Hold Short Operation (LAHSO)
    • When landing and takeoff are conducted simultaneously on intersecting runways, LAHSO may alert.
    • If receive and accept LAHSO, you must stop short of the intersecting runway prior to LAHSO signs and markings.
  • Taxiway Markings and Signs
    • Direction signs are normally located on left hand side of the taxiway and prior to the intersection
  • Enhanced Taxiway Centerline Markings
  • Destination Sign
    • Runway → numbers
    • Taxiway → alphabets
  • Holding Position Signs and Markings for an Instrument Landing System Critical Area
  • Holding Position Markings for Taxiway / Taxiway intersection
  • Permanently Closed Taxiway / Runway

Airport Lighting

  • Airport Beacon
  • Visual Approach Slope Indicator (VASI)
  • Precision Approach Path Indicator (PAPI)

Runway Lighting

  • Runway End Identifier Lights (REIL)
  • Runway Edge Lights

Wind Direction Indicators

  • Wind sock → indicated wind direction and wind velocity

Traffic patterns

  • A tetrahedron is installed to indicate the direction of landings and takeoffs. The small end of the tetrahedron points in the direction of landing
