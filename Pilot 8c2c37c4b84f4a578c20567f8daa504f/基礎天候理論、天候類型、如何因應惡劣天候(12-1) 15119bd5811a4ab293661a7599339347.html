Atmosphere

  • Composition of Atmosphere
    • 78% Nitrogen
    • 21% Oxygen
    • 1% others
  • Troposphere (對流層)
    • 6 ~ 20 km (4~12 miles)
    • Weather, cloud, storm and temperature
    • Decrease 2 degrees Celsius , one inch of air pressure every 1000 feet
  • Tropopause (對流層頂)
    • The location of jet stream and possible clean air turbulence (晴空亂流)
  • Stratosphere (平流層)
  • Mesosphere (中氣層)
  • Thermosphere (增溫層)

Atmospheric Circulation

  • The major factor is uneven heating of the Earth’s surface

Atmospheric Pressure

Coriolis Force (科氏力)

  • Northern Hemisphere right
  • Southern Hemisphere left

Measurement of Atmospheric Pressure

  • Measured in inches of mercury ("Hg) by a mercurial barometer (水銀汞柱)

Altitude and Flight

  • At higher altitude, the air pressure is lower than the ground level, and the takeoff lift is created by the flow of the air, when air pressure is lower, it means that there is not enough air surround the airfoil, thus the takeoff speed is higher and takeoff distance is longer

Wind and Current

  • Northern hemisphere
    • H → clockwise → good weather
    • L → counterclockwise → bad weather

Convective Current (對流)

  • Updraft occur at pavement or barren areas → quickly absorb and give off large amount of solar energy
  • Downdraft occur at water or forest → slowly absorb and give off small amount of solar energy
  • Sea breeze(morning) and land breeze(night)
  • Convective current affect pilot’s maneuver

Effect of Obstruction on Wind

  • Buildings and mountains will cause gusts

Low-level Wind Shear

  • Microburst (下擊暴流)

Wind and Pressure Representation on Weather Maps

Wind Representation

  • Arrow means where the wind comes from
  • Barbs(長方形) → 10 kts
  • Half barbs → 5 kts
  • Pennants(錦旗) → 50 kts

Pressure Representation

  • Isobar (等壓線)

Cloud Types

  • Low Clouds
    • stratus (層雲)
    • startocumulus
    • nimbostratus (雨層雲)
  • Middle Clouds
    • altostratus (高層雲)
    • altocumulus (高積雲)
  • High Clouds
    • cirrostratus (卷層雲)
    • cirrocumulus (卷積雲)
    • cirrus (卷雲)
  • Cumulonimbus (積雨雲) → the most dangerous cloud → could result in thunderstorm
  • Towering cumulus (濃積雲)

Fronts (鋒面)

  • Warm
    • Can create thunderstorm
    • move slowly 10 ~ 25 miles/hour
    • high humidity
    • light to moderate precipitation is possible

    Flight towards an approaching warm front

  • Cold
    • moves rapidly than warm fronts 25 ~ 30 mph
    • Weather changes after cold fronts pass a few hours
  • Stationary (滯留鋒)
  • Occluded (囚固鋒)
    • Cold front catches up slow-moving warm front
