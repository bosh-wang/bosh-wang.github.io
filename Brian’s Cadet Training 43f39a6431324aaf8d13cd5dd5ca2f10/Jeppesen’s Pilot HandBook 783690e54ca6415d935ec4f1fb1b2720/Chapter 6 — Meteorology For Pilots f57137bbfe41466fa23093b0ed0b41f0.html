Atmospheric Level

  • Troposphere → 36000 feet above ground

Composition of Atmosphere

Convection (對流)

Three Cell Circulation Pattern

  • Hadley Cell → hot air at equator rise and flow to higher latitude
  • Ferrel Cell → at 30 degree, air from Hadley Cell is cool enough to sink back to ground and flow to higher latitude
  • Polar Cell → air at pole position are cold to sink to ground and flow to lower latitude

Atmosphere Pressure

  • Isobar → 等壓線
  • Pressure gradient → change in pressure over distance
  • Ridge → high pressure
  • Trough → low pressure
  • Col → https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Col_(meteorology)

Coriolis Force

  • Higher latitude → greater effect on coriolis force

Frictional Force

  • However, within about 2,000 feet of the ground, friction caused by the earth's surface slows the moving air. This frictional force reduces the Coriolis force. Since the pressure gradient force is now greater than Coriolis force, the wind is diverted from its path along the isobars toward the lower pressure.
